Artists on Twitter Finder

"Artists on Twitter Finder" (AOTF) is an image search engine specialized in illustrations posted by artists on Twitter.

Enter the URL of an illustration's page on one of the supported websites, and the server will search the artist's Twitter accounts, as well as his Tweets containing this illustration.
